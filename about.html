The Snappers, as a club, have been around since 1997. After starting off in the city of Chicago, the club moved out to the Northwest Suburbs and came under new management. We practice and have home games at the Triphahn Ice Arena in the Hoffman Estates Park District. We welcome women at all levels of ability whether you are a beginner or you've played your entire life!

Proud member of the Women's Central Hockey League.
